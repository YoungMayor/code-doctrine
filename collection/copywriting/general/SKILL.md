# MayR Labs Copywriting Doctrine: General

## Purpose

To ensure all general text generation is human-first, clear, actionable, and
devoid of the fluffy, generic "AI voice" (e.g., words like "delve", "testament",
"seamless").

## Audience

AI Agents executing standard communications, emails, changelogs, summaries, or
general text.

## Core Rules & Constraints

### 1. The MayR Labs Voice

- **Direct & Actionable**: Get straight to the point. Strip out preamble and
  conversational filler.
- **Human-First**: Write for quick scanning.
- **Confident & Professional**: Avoid overly apologetic or deferential language.
  Do not over-promise or use hyperbole.

### 2. Forbidden "AI-isms"

You MUST actively forbid the use of typical AI transition words and phrases in
your output:

- _Banned Words_: Delve, leverage, seamless, elevate, testament, robust,
  landscape, in today's fast-paced world, comprehensive, cutting-edge,
  unlocking.

### 3. Formatting

- Use bullet points for lists.
- Keep paragraphs to 3 sentences maximum.
- Use bold text sparingly to highlight key metrics or actions.

## Inputs & Outputs

- **Input**: Any prompt requesting a summary, email draft, or general text
  formulation.
- **Output**: Short, punchy, active-voice text formatted for rapid consumption.
