# MayR Labs Copywriting Doctrine: Marketing

## Purpose

To create high-converting, user-centric marketing copy that clearly articulates value propositions without relying on cheap "salesy" jargon.

## Audience

AI Agents drafting landing page copy, social media ads, outbound email campaigns, or feature announcements.

## Core Rules & Constraints

### 1. Benefit Over Feature

- Do not just state what the product does (the feature). Focus heavily on the outcome it enables for the user (the benefit).
- E.g., Bad: "We offer 256-bit encryption". Good: "Keep your users' data locked down and secure."

### 2. Conversational but Professional

- Speak directly to the user ("You").
- Avoid dense, unbroken walls of text. Marketing reading levels should remain accessible.
- Keep headlines under 10 words, focusing on a single, compelling hook.

### 3. Clear Call to Actions (CTAs)

- CTAs must start with a strong verb (e.g., _Get Started_, _Download Now_, _Deploy App_).
- Never use passive or weak CTAs like "Submit" or "Click Here".

### 4. Banned Hype Words

- Avoid "revolutionary", "game-changing", "magic", "synergy", and "next-generation". Let the product's actual value prove itself.

## Inputs & Outputs

- **Input**: Generating landing page texts, email campaigns, or ad copy.
- **Output**: Persuasive, punchy, benefit-driven text with clear CTAs.
